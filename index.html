Frequently Asked Questions

How do I plan a meal?

Start by setting your target serving time. Then add each dish you're preparing with its cooking and prep times. The app automatically arranges everything on a timeline, showing you exactly when to start each step so everything finishes at the perfect time.

How do notifications work?

Cooking Time uses local notifications that run directly on your device. You'll receive alerts when it's time to start each cooking step. No internet connection required, and your data never leaves your device.

What's included in Pro?

Pro unlocks unlimited dishes and cooking steps (free version has limits), the ability to save and reuse your favorite meal timelines, and custom notification sounds. It's a one-time purchase with lifetime access.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

If you purchased Pro and need to restore it on a new device, open the app and tap the upgrade screen. At the bottom, you'll find a "Restore Purchases" button. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to make the purchase.

Can I save my favorite meal timelines?

Yes! With Cooking Time Pro, you can save unlimited meal collections. This is perfect for recurring dinners, holiday meals, or any combination of dishes you make regularly. Save once, reuse forever.

How accurate are the timers?

Cooking Time uses your device's system time, which is highly accurate. Timers continue to run even if you close the app, and notifications will trigger at the exact scheduled time.

Information We Collect

Cooking Time is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

How We Use Your Information

All cooking data, timelines, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect or store your personal information on our servers

  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking tools

Notifications

Cooking Time uses local notifications to alert you when it's time to start cooking steps. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not involve any data transmission.

In-App Purchases

Pro feature purchases are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not store your payment information. Purchase data is managed by Apple in accordance with their privacy policy.

Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. The app does not require an internet connection to function, and your cooking plans never leave your device unless you choose to back them up through iCloud (managed by Apple).

Children's Privacy

Cooking Time does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is designed for general audiences and does not include features directed at children under 13.
