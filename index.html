Frequently Asked Questions

How do I plan a meal?

Set your serving time, add dishes and durations, and the app builds the schedule automatically.

How do notifications work?

Notifications are local to your device and work even when the app is not open.

What is included in Pro?

Pro unlocks unlimited dishes and steps, saved collections, and custom alert sounds.

How do I restore my purchase?

Use the app's restore purchase option while signed in with the same Apple ID.

How accurate are timers?

Timers use your device system time and continue running when the app is backgrounded.
