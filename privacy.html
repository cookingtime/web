Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications published by Useful Products, including Cooking Time, Fractions Calculator Pro, and any other apps we develop. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our practices regarding data collection - or more precisely, our lack thereof.

Information We Do Not Collect

Our apps do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers such as your name, email address, phone number, or device ID
  • Location data
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information
  • Photos, contacts, or any other content from your device
  • Financial or payment information

No Account Required

Our apps do not require you to create an account or log in. You can use all features of our apps without providing any personal information.

No Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or other services that might collect data about you or your device.

Local Storage

Some of our apps may save preferences or data locally on your device (such as saved recipes or calculator history). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are suitable for general audiences and do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 or any other age group, as we do not collect information from any users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new effective date. Continued use of our apps after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
